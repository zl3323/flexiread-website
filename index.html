Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about TextTune

What is the CEFR standard?

The Common European Framework of Reference for Languages (CEFR) is an international standard for describing language ability on a six-point scale: A1 (Beginner), A2 (Elementary), B1 (Intermediate), B2 (Upper Intermediate), C1 (Advanced), and C2 (Proficient).

How does TextTune modify text?

TextTune adjusts content to be slightly above your selected CEFR level by:

  • Simplifying or replacing complex vocabulary while preserving key terms
  • Restructuring complex sentences into more digestible forms
  • Maintaining the original meaning and important information
  • Introducing a controlled amount of new vocabulary appropriate for your level
How can I best use TextTune for learning?

For maximum learning benefits:

  • Use authentic materials that interest you
  • Compare original and adjusted versions to see the differences
  • Focus on new vocabulary in the adjusted text
  • Gradually increase your CEFR level as you improve
